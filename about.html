How It Began

OUR STORY.

Redline Moto started as a dare between two engineers who couldn't find an adventure tour that matched their expectations. So they built one from scratch.

From a single bike and a hand-drawn map of the Spiti Valley, we've grown into India's most respected moto-adventure company — without ever compromising the spirit of the original ride.

2015

The First Ride

Two bikes. One map. The Spiti Valley in the dead of winter. Redline Moto was born on a 900km dare that nearly killed us — and made us obsessed.

2017

The Fleet Grows

We acquired our first fleet of 8 BMWs and KTMs, partnered with Castrol and Metzeler, and launched our first commercial Himalayan crossing with 12 riders.

2019

International Routes

Expanded beyond India — Bhutan, Nepal, and Sri Lanka entered the Redline route book. Welcomed our 1000th rider.

2022

Redline Academy

Launched India's first ADV rider training program — technical off-road courses taught by ex-rally co-drivers and mountain rescue specialists.

2025

4000+ Riders Guided

Ten years. Fifteen routes. Zero fatalities. Still the same obsession that started it all. The trail never ends.

What Drives Us

THE REDLINE CODE.

Four principles. No compromises. Every decision we make runs through this filter.

01

Safety First, Always

Adventure does not mean reckless. Every route is pre-ridden. Every bike is inspected daily. Our decade-long zero-fatality record is protocol.

02

Machine Perfection

We obsess over our fleet. Every bike is modified, tuned, and tested for specific terrain. If it isn't perfect, it doesn't leave the garage.

03

Brotherhood of Riders

Every rider who joins becomes part of the tribe. We don't just move in convoy — we watch each other's backs and share the summit.

04

Respect the Terrain

Low-impact riding and local partnerships ensure the mountains are here for the next generation. We leave no trace behind.
