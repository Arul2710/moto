Level-Specific Rules

REQUIREMENTS BY LEVEL.

In addition to core requirements, each difficulty tier has its own criteria. Find your level below.

Riding Experience

Minimum 1 year of active road riding. Must be comfortable on a bike above 200cc. No prior off-road experience required — but basic bike control at low speed is assessed at check-in.

Experience Required

Beginner Level

Physical Requirement

Able to ride 200–300km per day on mixed surfaces. Must be physically capable of lifting a 180kg bike from a horizontal position (assisted). Basic fitness — can walk 5km without difficulty.

  • 200–300km daily
  • Assisted bike lift
  • Basic walking fitness

Documents Needed

  • Valid motorcycle licence
  • Govt photo ID (Aadhaar / Passport)
  • Travel insurance (adventure cover)
  • Medical self-declaration form
  • Signed waiver of liability

Riding Experience

Minimum 3 years active riding with documented off-road or gravel experience. Must be able to demonstrate controlled off-camber cornering, emergency braking on loose surfaces, and bike recovery unaided.

Experience Required

Intermediate Level

Physical Requirement

Must be able to ride 300–400km on mixed terrain per day. Capable of independent bike recovery (no assist). Must be comfortable at altitude — prior trekking or cycling above 3,000m is strongly recommended.

  • 300–400km mixed terrain
  • Solo bike recovery
  • Altitude experience preferred

Additional Documents

  • All Beginner documents
  • Riding history log (3+ years)
  • Prior route or tour reference
  • Enhanced insurance (above 4,000m)

Riding Experience

Minimum 5 years ADV riding with at least one documented multi-day high-altitude expedition. Must complete our Skills Assessment Ride (SAR) — a 4-hour on-site evaluation at our Chennai facility before slot confirmation.

Experience Required

Expert Level

Physical Requirement

400–500km per day capability on extreme terrain. Independent full bike recovery. Altitude sickness history must be declared. GP sign-off letter strongly recommended for all Expert tier riders regardless of age.

  • 400–500km extreme terrain
  • SAR evaluation pass required
  • GP sign-off recommended

Expert-Only Documents

  • All Intermediate documents
  • SAR completion certificate
  • Altitude expedition proof
  • Inner-line permit application
  • Sat-phone rental confirmation

Cross-Border Paperwork

International routes (Bhutan, Nepal, Sri Lanka) require valid passport, visa, and carnet de passage or vehicle import permit. We handle permit logistics — riders must provide correct documents 60 days ahead of departure.

Language & Communication

Basic English is required to understand safety briefings. In remote regions, all emergency communication goes through our guides. An international SIM with roaming or a Redline-issued satellite communicator is mandatory from Day 5 onwards.

International Documents

  • All core requirements
  • Valid passport (6+ months)
  • Country-specific visa
  • International driving permit
  • Medical evacuation cover (intl)

WHAT GETS
YOU TURNED AWAY.

These are non-negotiable disqualifiers. No refund exceptions apply for bookings cancelled due to any of the following conditions at check-in.

If you're unsure whether any of these apply to you — contact us before booking, not after. Our guides would rather have that conversation than turn you away at the gate.

Under the Influence

Any rider presenting at the briefing or departure with detectable alcohol or substances is immediately removed from the tour. No exceptions, no second chances on that trip.

Missing or Expired Documents

Expired licence, missing insurance, or incomplete medical declaration at check-in results in immediate disqualification. No document, no ride — regardless of advance payment status.

Undisclosed Medical Conditions

If our medical officer uncovers a relevant undisclosed condition during the pre-ride check, the rider is grounded for their own safety and the safety of the group.

Failed Skills Assessment

Expert-tier riders who fail the SAR evaluation are offered a downgrade to a lower-difficulty tour at no extra cost. The original expert-tier slot is forfeited and refunded partially per policy.

Non-Compliant Safety Gear

Uncertified helmets (no ECE/ISI rating), missing armour in jacket or trousers, regular footwear, or bare-hand riding are all grounds for immediate disqualification. We offer loaner gear at an additional charge — but this is not guaranteed. Bring the right kit.
